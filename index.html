How I got my start

I began my coding journey in High School on CodeAcedemy working through the basics of HTML and CSS. I would spend hours in class coding - moving from module to module and I absolutely loved it. After High School I attempted to major in video game development but the curriculum was more desgin/art based and I lost interest quickly. Recently I've started up a business with my brother and I handle operations of everything and one of my many tasks is to build landing pages and sales pages. With the increasing fun I was experiencing I knew my only option was to pursue this career right away.

Thus leading me down my search of endless bootcamps and reading countless reviews. This is when a friend told me to check out Grand Circus. So I did. I quickly gained intrigue with perusing the website and scheduled a call. My 1:1 with Rachel was so enjoyable and it really solidified my want to be here. I stopped all communications with every other bootcamp I had and made my decision to enroll. I can't wait to see what the future has in store for me at Grand Circus and I am confident it will be a very bright one.
